Seattle Hot Spots

Sorry it took me so long to get you this darling, but here’s a list of spots you sould check out with your daddio while he’s in town!

Seattle Arboretum

The UW Aboretum / Botanical Gardens are amazing. On a Sunny day take a walk through the massive gardens / marsh there to catch some spectacular plant and wild life.

Madison Park Beach is really close too, so you could head down there afterwards. There’s also a few good places to eat down by the beach.

Garden Map

Google Map

Discovery Park

Discovery Park is breathtaking. It’s an old Military Base that was donated to the city. Now it’s a huge park with tons of beautiful scenery and hiking trails. I’d suggest checking out the beach.

Google Map

SAM Sculpture Park

The SAM sculpture park / Myrtle Edwards Park is a gorgeous park on the Seattle waterfront with an assortment of cool statues / art pieces. It seems like something your dad would really dig.

It’s right downtown, so I’d check it out after wandering out downtown for a bit. Plus the sunset is beautiful from there.

Google Map

The Whale Wins + Gasworks Park

Gasworks park is an old oil refinery that made into a park. Breathtaking views of the Seattle skyline and an intact old oil refinery make it a really cool park to check out.

After checking out the park take your dad to the resturant "The Whale Wins." It’s got amazing food and is within walking distance of the park.

P.S. I’m definitely going to smooch you sometime at Gasworks park on a clear night when we’ve got an impecable view of the city.
